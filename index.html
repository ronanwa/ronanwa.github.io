About

I am a research engineer investigating conservation technology in environmental monitoring and cultural preservation. Specifically, I am interested in (1) novel 3D modeling technqiues for documenting local climate impacts and (2) embedded sensor systems for data acquistion.

Keywords:   3D computer vision,   embedded systems,   spatial journalism.

Mentorship & Resources

Since 2018, I've mentored students in applying to programs like QuestBridge, DAAD RISE, NSF-REU, and Fulbright. Every year, I send reminders of application openings and deadlines, provide personal guidance in the application processes, offer feedback to essay statements, and share my successful/rejected applications in their entirety. In terms of research, I advise students in creating research projects that achieve personal growth and positive social impact while aligning with career development.

Recently, I joined QuestBridge's Mentorship Program as an alumni mentor. Having traversed these paths on my own before, I find fulfillment in developing personal relationships with other students, and offer myself as an open resource in research, academia, and career development. If you or anyone you know would benefit from this, please reach out via email or LinkedIn anytime.
